LIFE’S GOOD FILM

LG collaborated with young creators to spread the message of 'Life's Good' in the form of a short film
to shed light on today's creatives who are making a better tomorrow.
The Life's Good Film centers on the theme "diversity and limitless possibilities"
and brought a group of young creatives around the world together to share what makes life good for them.
